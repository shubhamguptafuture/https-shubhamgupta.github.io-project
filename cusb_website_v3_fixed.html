About Central University of South Bihar

A Parliament-established institution committed to academic excellence, inclusive growth & national service since 2009.

ज्ञान सेवा विमुक्तये — Knowledge, Service, Liberation

Central University of South Bihar (CUSB) was established by the Central Universities Act, 2009, passed by the Parliament of India. Located in Gaya — the sacred land of Buddha's enlightenment — CUSB stands as a beacon of higher education in eastern India.

Spread across 300 acres in Panchanpur, Gaya, the university offers comprehensive UG, PG, and Ph.D. programmes across 26 departments in sciences, social sciences, humanities, law, pharmacy, agriculture, and more.

Accredited by NAAC A++ (CGPA 3.58), CUSB is committed to its founding vision: making quality higher education accessible, fostering research, and serving the society — especially the underserved communities of Bihar.

Admissions 2025–26

All UG/PG admissions via CUET (NTA). Ph.D. via URET/NET. Contact: admission@cusb.ac.in · ☎ 0631-2229 530

Application Process

How to Apply

Step 01

Register & Appear in CUET

Visit cuet.samarth.ac.in, register for CUET-UG or CUET-PG conducted by NTA. All CUSB UG/PG admissions are based on CUET scores.

Step 02

Apply to CUSB Online

Visit cusb.ac.in admissions portal, fill the application, upload required documents, and pay the application fee online.

Step 03

Merit List & Counselling

CUSB publishes merit lists based on CUET scores. Shortlisted candidates attend online/offline counselling for seat allotment.

Step 04

Fee Payment & Enrolment

Pay admission fee within stipulated time, complete document verification, and confirm your seat at CUSB, Gaya.

Step 05 (Ph.D.)

URET / NET / GATE for Research

Ph.D. admissions via CUSB's University Research Entrance Test (URET) or direct for UGC-NET/CSIR-JRF/GATE qualified candidates.

Key Information

Eligibility & Dates

🎓 UG Eligibility

10+2 from any recognised board. Admission strictly via CUET-UG scores. Reservation: SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD as per GoI norms.

📚 PG Eligibility

Bachelor's with min. 55% (50% for SC/ST/PwD). Via CUET-PG. Subject-specific requirements — check individual department pages at cusb.ac.in.

🔬 Ph.D. Eligibility

PG with 55% (50% SC/ST/PwD). Admission via URET or direct for NET/JRF/GATE. Fellowships: UGC, CSIR, DST-INSPIRE, ICMR.
